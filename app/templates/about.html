Welcome to Stock Market Price Trend Prediction and Backtesting Strategy System.

Stock Market Price Trend Prediction and Backtesting Strategy System is a comprehensive, AI driven platform engineered to deliver advanced stock market analytics, predictive modelling, and portfolio intelligence for participants across the financial ecosystem.

System Features

Customer Features

  • ✔ Register and create an account with email verification.
  • ✔ Portfolio Management: add, view, delete or modify stocks in holdings.
  • ✔ Search and view price chart, fundamental analysis of stocks along with historical prices.
  • ✔ A screener tool that filters companies using queries with multiple parameters and conditions, displaying results in a table.
  • ✔ Make predictions of a stock based on the target date selected along with validation.
  • ✔ Add or view feedback and response from the admin.
  • ✔ News section to view latest news along with AI summary of market sentiments.
