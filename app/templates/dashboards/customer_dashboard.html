📊 How to Use Stock Screener
What is a Stock Screener?
A stock screener helps you filter and find stocks based on specific criteria. Write queries using stock parameters to discover stocks that match your investment strategy.
Available Parameters (16 total)
  • price or current_price - Current stock price (₹)
  • market_cap - Market capitalization
  • pe_ratio or pe - Price to Earnings ratio
  • pb_ratio or pb - Price to Book ratio
  • volume - Trading volume
  • dividend_yield or dividend - Dividend yield %
  • roe - Return on Equity %
  • roa - Return on Assets %
  • debt_to_equity - Debt to Equity ratio
  • beta - Stock volatility
  • eps - Earnings Per Share
  • profit_margin - Profit margin %
  • revenue_growth - Revenue growth %
  • earnings_growth - Earnings growth %
  • current_ratio - Current ratio (liquidity)
  • peg_ratio - PEG ratio
Operators
  • > - Greater than
  • < - Less than
  • = - Equal to
  • AND - Both conditions must be true
  • OR - Either condition must be true
Example Queries
market_cap > 100000 AND pe_ratio < 20
Find large cap stocks with low P/E ratio
roe > 15 AND debt_to_equity < 1
Find profitable stocks with low debt
dividend_yield > 0.03 AND beta < 1
Find high dividend low volatility stocks
pb_ratio < 3 AND current_ratio > 1.5
Find undervalued stocks with good liquidity
earnings_growth > 0.2 AND pe_ratio < 25
Find growth stocks at reasonable valuations
Tips
  • Start with simple queries and add more criteria gradually
  • Use AND to narrow down results, OR to broaden them
  • Market cap values are in crores (1 crore = 10 million)
  • Click on any stock in results to view detailed charts
